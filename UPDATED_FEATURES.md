## 🚀 How to Use

### For Faculty:

1. **Create Subject**
   - Go to "Subjects" in sidebar
   - Click "Create Subject" button
   - Fill in the form
   - Click "Create Subject"
   - **It will automatically refresh and show your new subject!**

2. **Create Units**
   - Click on "Units" button on any subject card
   - Click "Create Unit" button
   - Fill in the form
   - Click "Create Unit"
   - List refreshes automatically

3. **Create Topics** (Next feature to add)
   - Coming soon...

4. **Create Exam**
   - Go to "Exams" in sidebar
   - Click "Create Exam" button (NOW WORKING!)
   - Fill in all the form fields
   - Click "Create Exam"
   - Navigate to exam list

### For Students:

1. **View Exams**
   - Login as student
   - Go to "My Exams" in sidebar
   - See all assigned exams

2. **Take Exam**
   - Click "Take Exam" button
   - Answer all questions
   - Submit your exam
