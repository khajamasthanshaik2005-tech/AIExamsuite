## 📚 Faculty Workflow

### 1. Create Subject
- Click "Subjects" in sidebar
- Click "Create Subject" button
- Fill form:
  - Code: `CSE101`
  - Name: `Machine Learning`
  - Semester: `Fall 2024`
  - Department: `Computer Science`
  - Description: `Introduction to ML`
- Click "Create Subject"
- **It will appear immediately in the list!**

### 2. Create Units
- Click "Units" button on any subject card
- Click "Create Unit" button
- Fill form:
  - Title: `Introduction to ML`
  - Description: `Basic concepts`
  - Estimated Hours: `20`
- Click "Create Unit"

### 3. Create Topics (In Units page)
- After selecting a unit, you'll see topics
- Can add topics if needed
- Topics are selected for exams

### 4. Create Exam
- Click "Exams" → "Create Exam"
- **Select Subject** (dropdown works!)
- **Select Units** (buttons work!)
- **Select Topics** (checkboxes work!)
- Fill other details:
  - Title, Type, Duration, Marks, etc.
  - Start/End time
  - Instructions
- **Two options:**
  - **Manual**: Click "Create Exam" (creates empty exam)
  - **AI Generation**: Click "Generate with AI" (creates + generates questions)

### 5. Assign to Students
- After creating exam, you can assign it
- Students will see it in their dashboard
